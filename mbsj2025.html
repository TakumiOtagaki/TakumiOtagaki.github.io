Notes (Preparation & Updates)

I ended up pushing myself pretty hard and finally finished preparing the poster on November 30. I really don’t want to be in a situation where I have to work that hard right before a conference if I can help it. Right before a conference, I always feel this urge to somehow make the results look better, or to push the research just a bit further, and I end up overworking. But it reminded me once again that research is something you really need to move forward with steadily every day (even though I feel like I tell myself the same thing before every conference).
